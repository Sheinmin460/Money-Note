# 🚀 MoneyNote Features

Explore the powerful capabilities of **MoneyNote**, your personal finance and project tracking companion.

---

## 📊 Dashboard & Analytics

The heart of MoneyNote is its clean, modern dashboard that provides an instant overview of your financial health.

- **Real-Time Totals**: Instantly see your total **Income**, **Expenses**, **Profit**, and overall **Balance**.
- **Modern Interface**: A sleek, card-based UI that works beautifully on both desktop and mobile.
- **Recent Activity**: A clear, chronological list of your latest transactions with quick edit and delete options.

## 📈 Admin Dashboard & Insights

For a higher-level view, the Admin Dashboard offers visual data representations to help you compare performance across different projects.

- **Project Comparison Graphs**: Integrated bar charts that visualize **Expense**, **Income**, and **Profit** across all your projects.
- **Data-Driven Decisions**: Easily identify which projects are most profitable and where your resources are being allocated.

## 📁 Project Management

Organize your transactions into logical groups using our robust project system.

- **Dedicated Summaries**: Each project has its own dashboard showing specific totals and transaction history.
- **Status Tracking**: Keep track of ongoing and completed ventures.
- **Seamless Integration**: Link transactions to projects with a single click during creation or editing.

## 🤝 Collaboration System

MoneyNote is built for teams and shared finances.

- **Invite Collaborators**: Share any project by inviting other users via their email address.
- **Secure Access**: Invitations require password confirmation for added security.
- **Collaborator Roles**: View a list of all participants in a project. Owners can manage access and remove collaborators as needed.
- **In-App Notifications**: Receive alerts on your home screen whenever you're invited to a new project.

## 🆕 Transaction Approval Workflow

Maintain control over project finances with a built-in approval system.

- **Pending Transactions**: Transactions added to collaborative projects can be marked as "Pending".
- **Review & Approve**: Project owners can review these transactions directly from the Project Detail page.
- **One-Click Actions**: Quickly approve or reject pending items to keep your records accurate.

## 💳 Wallet & Credit Management

Flexible wallet options to match your real-world financial setup.

- **Multiple Wallet Types**: Support for **Cash**, **Bank**, and **Credit** accounts.
- **Custom Credit Limits**: Create specialized credit wallets with user-defined negative balance limits.
- **Smart Validation**: The system automatically prevents transactions that would exceed your set credit limit.
- **Automatic Updates**: Renaming a wallet instantly updates all historical transactions associated with it.

## 💸 Transfer System

Move money between your wallets without distorting your income and expense reports.

- **Atomic Transfers**: Move funds from one wallet to another in a single, secure operation.
- **Dedicated Transfer Logs**: A separate history page allows you to track all internal movements of capital clearly.
- **Limit Checks**: Transfers also respect your credit limits for maximum financial safety.

## 🔍 Advanced Filtering & Categorization

Find exactly what you're looking for with powerful search and filter tools.

- **Mandatory Categorization**: Ensures all transactions are organized for clear reporting.
- **Smart Filters**: Filter by category (including handling for uncategorized/NULL items), search by description, or set custom date ranges.
- **Reporting Ready**: Clean, categorized data makes it easy to export or analyze your spending habits.

## 🔐 User Authentication & Security

Your data is private and secure.

- **Personal Accounts**: Each user has a completely private workspace.
- **JWT-Based Security**: Secure, industry-standard authentication ensures only you can access your data.
- **Data Integrity**: Uses SQLite transactional updates to ensure Consistency even during complex data operations.

## ✨ Modern UI/UX

A premium experience designed for speed and ease of use.

- **Skeleton Loading**: Smooth, animated loading states prevent UI "jumping" and provide a polished feel.
- **Responsive Design**: Built with Tailwind CSS to look great on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
- **Intuitive Icons**: Powered by Lucide React for a beautiful and consistent visual language.
